Frequenty Asked Questions

What is this app?

This app is a tool designed to help students prepare for exams by generating AI-powered quizzes based on the topics they want to study.

How does the app generate quizzes?

The app uses advanced artificial intelligence to analyze the topics you input and create questions that test your understanding of those subjects. These questions can include multiple-choice, true/false, short answer, and more.

Is the app free to use?

The app is currently free to use, but some premium features may be introduced in the future.

What types of questions does the app support?

The app supports multiple-choice questions, true/false, short answer.

I found an error in a quiz question. What should I do?

If you notice an error, you can try generating the quiz again as sometimes the AI-generated content may be unexpected.
